ABOUT US

SUFNECH represents "Support for Needy Children," focusing on empowering underprivileged children, particularly orphans and vulnerable Children (OVCs) in Migori County, Kenya. Migori County faces significant poverty challenges, particularly affecting children, women, and the elderly. SUFNECH is committed to providing holistic care and support to underprivileged children in the region.

What We Do

Sufnech is a dedicated charity organization based in Migori, committed to improving the lives of vulnerable communities in our region. Founded on the principles of compassion, empowerment, and sustainability, Sufnech strives to create lasting change by addressing the pressing needs of those who are often overlooked. At Sufnech, we are more than just a charity; we are a community-driven movement that brings together passionate volunteers, generous donors, and local partners to make a real difference. Our efforts are guided by transparency, integrity, and a deep respect for the people we serve

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide essential support through various initiatives, including education, healthcare, food security, and community development. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to lead a dignified life, and we work tirelessly to ensure that help reaches those who need it most.

Our Vission

We envision a vibrant community where each child has the opportunity to lead a healthy, educated, and prosperous life

OUR Services

We provide essential services to uplift our community, including education for underprivileged children, food security through meal distributions, and access to healthcare. We also support sustainable livelihoods and empower small-scale entrepreneurs, all with the aim of fostering self-reliance and lasting positive impact in Migori.
